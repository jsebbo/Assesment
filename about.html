About Us

How You Can Help

Anyone can help GAIA in a lot of ways. Such as volunteering for our daily litter pickings, or even help start a fundraiser to help raise funds for our causes. Whatever it may be, every little helps. We also host tree planting, to ensure our forrests are well maintained and full as trees are esential in life! You can be any age to help on volunteers with GAIA. Anyone under the age of 16 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

Environmental Issues

At GAIA, we focus on all environmental issues. But we specifically focus on:

  • Recycling

  • Weather

  • Deserts

  • The North and South Poles

We really want to make a difference in the world so please see our volunteering section for how you can help us.

Our Workers

Our workers and volunteers work hard around the clock every day. They all have the same mission, which is to make our planet a better place and to keep it that way for the rest of time.

What We Do

GAIA is an environmental association which helps highlight global environmental issues that are a threat to the worlds ecosystem. GAIA aims to give solutions to these issues to the public so everyone can make the world better for us and future generations to come.
