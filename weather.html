About Weather

Weather is of course a crucial part of our ecosystem. Many of our wildlife and beings need the sun, rain, snow, cold and heat to survive and thrive. But the weather can also have a severe impact on our environment, with global warming from toxic fumes getting in the air which causes ice caps to melt, resulting in our water levels slowly rising causing floods and making life uninhabitabal for wildlife such as polar bears!

Volunteering
