Deserts

About Deserts

With the increasing heat in deserts, this is causing wildfires within the wild shrubs and trees. Seeing as deserts are so hot, the trees, shrubs and any sort of plants are extremely dry, making it extremely easy for wildfires to occur and pollute our air.

Desertification is also a massive issue within our deserts. This is where people take down any trees or wildlife in deserts, making them uninhabitable for any sort of wildlife to live and thrive.
