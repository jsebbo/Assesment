North and South Poles

About The Poles

The North Pole is the most northern point on the planet. It is located at the intersection of earths axis and earths surface. There is also the South Pole which is the most southern part of the earth. These poles help to create our magnetic field.

Volunteering

You can help us with volunteering by signing up with us and helping our research on how these poles are crucial for our earth and how to prevent any damage and pollution to the areas.

How To Help

To help preserve wildlife in our poles, our advice is not to litter! Bits of plastic found in the ice can effect the wildlife, potentially killing animals such as polar bears. Another way to help is to stop any air pollution as this contributes to global warming, which is starting to melt all the ice.
