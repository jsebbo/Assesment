Recycling

About Recycling

Recycling is the practice of converting waste that has been thrown out back into new material to use on new items. This is such as using old plastic bottles, melting them down and then making that melted plastic into new plastic bottles. This helps the environment as it is using materials that are already in this world, rather than making new material and it being littered across our planet and countrysides.

Volunteering

Help us by signing up to our litter picking program. These are based all across the world! We have daily litter pickings which last approx two hours each, where we all get into large groups, spread out and pick any litter off our streets, making progress each time to a greener earth!

How To Help

To help us here at GAIA on our mission, we ask you to recycle any time you can! Finished with your coke bottle? Recycle it! It may not seem like much, but you will actually be playing a big part in helping our world be litter free and more eco friendly!
